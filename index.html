This was the first android application that I developed. It calculates A levels rank points for students based on their grades for each subject. I also wrote a script with Beautiful Soup in Python to scrape the IGP cut off for NUS, NTU and SMU. I then compare the students' rank points with IGP for each course and display the courses that students are eligible for. If the students miss the IGP of a course by a narrow margin, the course will still be displayed. Also integrated Google Admob to run ads. The app has over 500 downloads to date and it is available in the Google Playstore for free.
